Banana Bread

Description

itchen with a sweet, comforting aroma as it bakes. Made with ripe, mashed bananas, this moist and tender loaf has a rich, natural sweetness that makes every slice irresistible. The bananas are the star ingredient, lending both flavor and moisture to the bread, while a hint of vanilla and a dash of cinnamon add warmth and depth. The batter is often enriched with butter or oil, giving the bread a soft, cake-like texture that melts in your mouth. Once baked to a golden brown, the bread develops a slightly crisp crust that contrasts beautifully with the soft, dense interior. Some versions include add-ins like chopped nuts or chocolate chips, adding extra layers of flavor and texture. Banana bread is perfect for breakfast, a snack, or even dessert, offering a satisfying bite that’s both familiar and comforting, with just the right balance of sweetness and heartiness.

Ingredients

  • Flour: All-purpose flour gives the banana bread structure 2 cups all-purpose flour.
  • Baking soda: Baking soda acts as a leavener, which means it helps the banana bread rise. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Salt: A pinch of salt enhances the overall flavor, but it won't make the loaf taste salty. ¼ teaspoon salt
  • Butter: A stick of butter lends richness, moisture, and irresistible flavor. ½ cup butter
  • Brown sugar: Brown sugar sweetens things up and adds a hint of warmth.¾ cup brown sugar
  • Eggs: Eggs act as a binding agent, which means they hold the batter together. 2 large eggs, beaten
  • Bananas: Of course, you'll need bananas! Choose overripe bananas. 2 ⅓ cups mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed bananas.
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture.
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven
  5. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  6. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Beat brown sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a separate large bowl until smooth. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until just combined. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 60 minutes. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
