The best lasagna ever

Description

Lasagna is like a warm, comforting hug in the form of food. Imagine layers of flat pasta sheets stacked on top of each other, with each layer holding a rich and savory filling. The filling is often a mix of ground meat, like beef or sausage, cooked in a tangy tomato sauce. Some versions include creamy ricotta cheese, which adds a soft, velvety texture. These layers are topped with a generous amount of melted cheese, usually mozzarella, which becomes golden and bubbly when baked. The result is a hearty, flavorful dish with a perfect balance of textures—soft pasta, tender meat, and gooey cheese—all mingling together in every bite. Lasagna is a dish that fills you up and leaves you feeling satisfied, with each forkful offering a taste of home and tradition.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce 2 cans
  • Tomato Paste 2 cans
  • Crushed Tomato 1 can
  • Sugar 2 Tablespoons
  • spices and seasoning, fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, Salt, Italian seasoning,fennel seeds and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles, can use store bought or homemade
  • Cheeses, Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • An egg

Steps

  1. Make The meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make The ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
